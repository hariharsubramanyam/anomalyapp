Anomaly App

This application let's you monitor for atypical behavior in multiple video streams.

There are 8 video streams grouped into 2 clusters of 4. When zoomed in, the individual streams are represented by markers. When zoomed out, the streams are condensed into 2 markers, one per cluster. Click on the marker to view the associated stream.

The application takes some time to launch the streams, so please be patient. It has completed launching the streams when the markers begin to be periodically resized according to their anomaly level.
